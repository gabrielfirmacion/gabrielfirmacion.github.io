A short story about my path to a coding life

When I moved to the land of America about 2 years ago, I saw the power and beauty of code. I dreamed of apps that I wanted to create that could hopefully be the next big thing. Unfortunately, I did not know how to code. So I decided to go to Dev Bootcamp, an immersive program that teaches you Ruby on Rails and JavaScript. It was a life changing experience! Coding day and night for 18 weeks was all worth it. Dev Bootcamp gave me the proper foundation in order to learn and understand new technologies on my own. Everyday including the day you are reading this, I am either creating an app for myself or for others.

Thank you for visiting my website. I hope you like it! I could have told you more stories but I have to get back to coding. Just like any method in Ruby, I need to 'end' this.
